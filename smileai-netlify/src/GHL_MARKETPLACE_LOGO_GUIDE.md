# 🎨 GoHighLevel Marketplace App Logo Guide

## Overview
Your app logo is the first thing users see in the GHL Marketplace. It needs to be professional, recognizable, and meet GHL's technical requirements.

---

## 📐 Technical Requirements

### Image Specifications
- **Format**: PNG (preferred) or JPG
- **Dimensions**: **512x512 pixels** (square)
- **File Size**: Under 1MB
- **Background**: Transparent PNG recommended
- **Color Mode**: RGB
- **Resolution**: 72-144 DPI

### Alternative Sizes Needed
1. **App Icon**: 512x512px (main marketplace listing)
2. **Thumbnail**: 256x256px (search results, grid view)
3. **Small Icon**: 128x128px (installed apps list)
4. **Favicon**: 64x64px (browser tab)

---

## 🎯 Design Guidelines

### Brand Identity for "SmileVision AI"

#### Core Elements:
1. **Smile/Teeth Symbol**: Central to the brand
2. **Modern/Tech Feel**: Represents AI transformation
3. **Professional Medical**: Dental clinic credibility
4. **Clean & Simple**: Works at small sizes

#### Color Palette:
- **Primary**: Blue/Cyan (#00A6E8 or similar)
  - Represents trust, professionalism, medical
- **Accent**: White/Light (#FFFFFF)
  - Clean, dental, bright smile
- **Secondary**: Purple/Gradient (optional)
  - Modern, AI, technology
- **Avoid**: Yellow/Gold (can look dated)

#### Typography:
- **Modern Sans-Serif** (if text included)
- **Bold/Semibold weight**
- **Readable at small sizes**
- **Minimal text** (icon should work alone)

## 🖼️ Visual Style Recommendations

### What Works Well:
✅ **Icon-only designs** (scalable, recognizable)
✅ **2-3 colors max** (clean, professional)
✅ **Circular or rounded square shape** (modern)
✅ **Subtle gradients** (depth without clutter)
✅ **Negative space** (smile shape in circle)
✅ **Simple geometric shapes** (tooth, smile arc)
✅ **Small sparkle/star accent** (transformation theme)

### What to Avoid:
❌ Text-heavy designs (illegible at small sizes)
❌ Complex illustrations (loses detail when scaled)
❌ More than 3-4 colors (looks busy)
❌ Thin lines (invisible at 64px)
❌ Realistic photos (doesn't scale well)
❌ Drop shadows (looks outdated)
❌ 3D effects (marketplace trend is flat)

## 💡 Pro Tips

### 1. Test at Multiple Sizes
Export your logo at:
- 512px (marketplace)
- 128px (grid view)
- 64px (list view)
- 32px (favicon)

Does it still look good? If not, simplify.

### 2. Use Negative Space
A smile can be created with:
- A curve on a circle (cut-out style)
- White space between colored shapes
- Minimal lines with maximum impact

### 3. Color Psychology
- **Blue**: Trust, medical, professional ✅
- **Purple**: Innovation, premium, AI ✅
- **Green**: Health, growth (alternative)
- **White**: Clean, dental, pure ✅
- **Red**: Avoid (emergency, danger)

### 4. Trademark Check
Before finalizing, search:
- Google Images (reverse image search)
- USPTO Trademark Database
- Similar dental app logos
