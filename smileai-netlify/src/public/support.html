❓ Common Questions

Q: How do I get my GHL API credentials?

Go to GoHighLevel → Settings → API → Create API Key. Copy the key and your Location ID from Settings → Business Profile.

Q: Where are the videos saved in GHL?

Videos are saved in two places: (1) Custom field smile_video_url and (2) Contact Notes with a clickable link.

Q: What custom fields do I need?

You need 5 custom fields: service_interest, transformation_status, before_image_url, after_image_url, and smile_video_url. See the setup guide for details.

Q: Why aren't contacts being created in GHL?

Check that your API Key and Location ID are correctly saved in Settings → Integration. Verify your API key has contacts.write permission.

Q: How long does video generation take?

Video generation typically takes 3-5 minutes. The customer will see a progress indicator while the video is being created.

Q: Can I customize the branding?

Yes! Go to Staff Login → Settings → Branding to customize your clinic name, colors, logo, and more.
